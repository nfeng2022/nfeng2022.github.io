My dissertation is on applications of stochastic methods and machine learning techniques in computational mechanics and topology optimization.

My research interests are data-driven computational mechanics, uncertainty quantification and robust topology optimization.  Focusing on speeding up computation analysis of complex mechanics systems, I developed efficient and accurate surrogate models and computational frameworks in various mechanics applications such as forward stochastic problems, multiscale analysis, and inverse robust design optimization considering uncertainties.
